Hosting

The room. Together.

Hosting is its own craft. Half conductor, half guide, half stand-up. Sean has hosted festivals, panels, awards, podcasts, and live community events at every scale.

  • 01Festivals.Multi-day, multi-stage. Vegans Explore tour included. Comfortable on outdoor mainstage and intimate side rooms.
  • 02Conferences and panels.Moderating, MC, panel host. Tech, education, community, and brand events.
  • 03Awards and ceremonies.Scripted segments plus live banter. Production-friendly. Hits the cue.
  • 04Live podcasts.In-studio or stage-cast. Long-form interviews. Pre-show and post-show production included.
