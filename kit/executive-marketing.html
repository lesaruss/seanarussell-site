ABOUT

Sean A. Russell is a marketing and media executive with twenty years building brand, content, and operations systems from the ground up, most recently as the strategist behind an $800M voter-approved bond campaign for the sixth-largest school district in the United States.

He is the founder and CEO of Lesaruss Inc., a media and marketing company he has led since 2007, and the architect of LESARUSS, an AI-powered production system that lets a small team run a full marketing department for organizations of any size.

As District Webmaster for Broward County Public Schools, Sean led the year-long integrated campaign behind an $800,000,000 voter-approved bond, serving as Lead Webmaster, video producer, and web migration lead across 75 departments and 200+ schools.

His client work spans industries: growing LashMakers, a global beauty brand, from $167K to $262K+ in quarterly revenue, a 10.16x return on ad spend; taking Balance for Life Florida, a wellness retreat, from $12.9K to $107K in quarterly revenue, 730% growth; and building South Florida's largest Vegan community and media channel over 10+ years.

Sean is available for full-time executive marketing roles, remote only, or as a Fractional CMO: he becomes an organization's chief marketing officer, running its full stack of content, brand, and campaigns on LESARUSS's AI-powered systems, backed by community managers who handle the human side of every relationship. The model is built to scale: as client organizations grow, so does the team behind them, coordinators and additional CMOs brought on as needed, all compounding off the same body of case studies and best practices.
