About Sean

Producer. Food Culture Creator. Systems Builder.

Sean A. Russell is a producer, media strategist, and community builder with over two decades of experience at the intersection of food culture, storytelling, live production, and digital ecosystems. He is the founder of Vegans Explore, a nationally recognized plant-based food and lifestyle platform that has grown into a multi-city community of events, content, partnerships, and audience engagement.

Through Vegans Explore, Sean has produced live food and culture events, coordinated brand partnerships, developed content strategies for plant-based audiences, and built the community infrastructure that connects food-focused creators and consumers across eight launch cities. His work is grounded in genuine love for plant-based food culture, which means he brings authentic voice and community trust to every project.

Earlier in his career, Sean produced hundreds of artist interviews, convention coverage, and live event media through Anime 3000 and Top Spot USA, developing field production instincts and logistics experience across music, entertainment, and fandom cultures. His production work generated over one million podcast downloads and earned industry relationships with Crunchyroll and Nintendo.

Sean also spent years at Broward County Public Schools building communication systems, student media programs, and district-wide engagement frameworks, developing the operational discipline and cross-team coordination that makes productions run under pressure. What he brings to any production is a producer's mindset: he thinks in systems, communicates across teams, and cares deeply about the experience the audience takes home.
