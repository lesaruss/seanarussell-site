The Book.

What it answers.

Why go vegan. How. What’s in it for you.

Pre-Vegans is built around a simple observation: the questions people actually ask before going vegan rarely get a straight answer. This book is a set of real conversations with people who have lived every side of that journey, told so someone standing at the very start can find their own way in.
